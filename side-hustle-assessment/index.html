High-level Side Hustle Assessment Grades

The Home Office Politics side hustle assessment is intended to help you as an entrepreneur to understand your preparedness to start--or continue with--a side hustle.

Please complete (or retake) the high-level side hustle assessment for entrepreneurs if you have not already.

The results of this evaluation is intended for informational purposes only; but knowledge is power.

Instructions: After you completed the 60-second high-level side hustle assessment for entrepreneurs, you were redirected to this page and to the correct section that applies to your own evaluation. We also emailed a copy of your score to your valid email address that you provided at the end of the survey. You can reference that email if necessary to find your score again. You can also refresh the page and you will be re-directed back to your score section.

Side Hustle Preparedness Grades

  • Grade A: Excellent
  • Grade B: Good
  • Grade C: Low
  • Grade D: Bad
  • Grade F: Terrible

These grades range from 0-31, zero being low and 31 being high.

Grade A: Excellent

An excellent grade are ranges from 23-31 (maximum).

Grade B: Good

16-22

Grade C: Low

10-15

Grade D: Bad

3-9

Grade F: Terrible

0-2
